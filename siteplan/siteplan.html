Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Glam Nails Mobile Studio

This name was selected because it clearly identifies the business as a professional mobile nail service that provides beauty treatments at the client's location.

Optional Domain: glamnailsmobile.co.za

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about mobile nail services, pricing, service packages, business contact information, appointment booking options, and a gallery of completed nail work. Visitors will be able to learn about available services and request appointments online.

Scenarios

  • What nail services are available and what do they cost?
  • How can I book an appointment for a mobile nail technician?
  • What areas does the business serve?
  • Can I view examples of previous nail designs before booking?

Color Scheme

  • #D266B0 (Pink) - Headings, buttons, highlights, and navigation accents.
  • #F8E8F3 (Light Pink) - Page background and content sections.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray) - Body text and footer content.

Typography

  • Poppins - Used for headings and navigation.
  • Arial - Used for body text and forms.
